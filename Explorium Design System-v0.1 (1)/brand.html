Voice

We are

  • Confident and specific — we lead with outcomes, not adjectives.
  • Technical when it helps, plain when it doesn’t.
  • Signal over noise — every sentence earns its place.

We aren’t

  • Buzzword-heavy. No “synergy”, no “disrupt”, no “game-changer”.
  • Hype-driven — claims are backed by numbers, sources or customers.
  • Bloated — cut a word if the sentence still works.
