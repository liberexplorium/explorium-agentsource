# Explorium Design System

Digital design system for Explorium — B2B data and infrastructure for AI-SDR and GTM teams. Extracted from the production Figma (`Design-System-2025`) and the public website.

## Content fundamentals

**Mission.** Setting a new standard for the data-driven world — giving revenue teams reliable, enriched, predictive data to power AI-led GTM motions.

**Voice.**
- Confident, specific, outcome-led (“cut invalid contacts by 76%” — not “transform your pipeline”).
- Technical when it helps; plain when it doesn’t.
- Short sentences. No buzzwords (“synergy”, “disrupt”, “game-changer”).
- Claims are always backed by a number, a source or a customer.

**Core vocabulary.** Data, signals, enrichment, connectivity, predictive prospecting, GTM agents, AI-SDR, pipeline.

## Visual foundations

**Palette.** Cream surface (`#FFFCF5`), deep teal ink (`#004349`), hairline teal (`#C4DBE1`). Lime (`#D1EE71`) is the only CTA fill. Coral, indigo, mint, blush are accents — used sparingly, one per moment.

**Type.** Gilroy across the whole system. Display & H1 in Regular (400) with `-2%` tracking; H3/H4/Title in Medium (500); CTAs in Bold (700) with `+5%` tracking. Space Mono for API / code moments only.

**Shape.** Pill-first geometry — buttons, nav, inputs all use `border-radius: 60px`. Cards use 16–24px. Generous white space over decoration.

**Hairlines, not dividers.** Thin `#C4DBE1` lines separate sections; avoid heavy borders.

**Imagery.** Photographic screenshots of the product over illustrations; faint line-grid backgrounds in hero moments. No gradients in brand surfaces.

- `brand.html` — logo, clear space, favicon, voice
- `fonts/` — Gilroy (Light → Heavy, incl. italics)
- `assets/` — logo and favicon
